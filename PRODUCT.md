# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Primary audience is twofold: (1) logistics clients — importers, exporters, and freight forwarders across the Arab world who need reliable international road transport; and (2) fleet partners — truck owners and drivers seeking a professional, trustworthy operator. Both visit the site seeking confidence in capability, reach, and reliability.

## Product Purpose

Al-Shaheebi Trust International Road Transport is a premium logistics company connecting Arab capitals and industrial zones via road. The site exists to establish authority, showcase geographic coverage across 15 Arab capitals, communicate fleet capability, and convert visitors into clients or partners. Success is measured by trust earned and inquiries generated.

## Brand Personality

Trusted, Premium, Powerful. The brand carries itself with quiet authority — no hype, no gimmicks. It is established, confident, and deeply rooted in the Arab world.

## Anti-references

- No generic trucking sites: avoid cheap stock photography, clip art, dated layouts, or anything that feels like a template.
- No cold corporate templates: the site should feel connected to the Middle East, not transplanted from a Western corporate playbook.

## Design Principles

- **Middle East pride**: Celebrate Arab world heritage and geography. The coverage section — 15 Arab capitals — is a centerpiece, not an afterthought.
- **Cinematic authority**: Video backgrounds, dark premium tones, and dramatic visuals convey scale and capability.
- **Understated excellence**: The design does the work quietly. No loud CTAs, no hype patterns, no hero-metric clichés.
- **Show, don't tell**: Visual proof of fleet, coverage, and operations over written claims.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA compliance target
- prefers-reduced-motion respected throughout
- RTL layout for Arabic content
